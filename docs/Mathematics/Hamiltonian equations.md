Generalised momentum
$$p=\frac{\partial T}{\partial \dot q}$$
Hamiltonian
$$H=\sum p\dot q-L=\sum\frac{\partial T}{\partial \dot q}\dot q-L$$
$$\boxed{\dot q=\frac{\partial H}{\partial p}\quad -\dot p=\frac{\partial H}{\partial q}}$$
